ABOUT

The Zannah Reporters (ZR) is a dynamic and youth-driven news and publicity organization dedicated to documenting, promoting, and broadcasting the impactful works, projects, programs, events, and activities of Hon. Abdulmalik Zubairu (Zannah Bungudu).

The Movement is driven by thousands of passionate youths from various sectors who believe in the transformative leadership and people-centered governance of Hon. Zannah. We serve as a bridge between his achievements and the public, ensuring that every project, community engagement, and developmental initiative receives the visibility and recognition it deserves.

Through our media platforms, we aim to inspire civic engagement, promote transparency, and strengthen community awareness about the positive changes taking place under his representation.

The Movement is backed by thousands of youths from across Zamfara State, united by a common goal to amplify the voice and vision of a leader who truly represents the hopes and aspirations of his people.

HON. (Dr.) ABDULMALIK ZUBAIRU

GRAND PATRON

Hon. (Dr.) Abdulmalik Zubairu, also known as Zannah Bungudu, is the current Member representing Maru/Bungudu federal constituency at the 10th National Assembly in Abuja. Born on September 27, 1978, in Bungudu, Zamfara State, he has a rich educational background, including a Bachelor's degree in Political Science from Bayero University Kano and a Master's degree in International Affairs and Diplomacy from Usman Danfodio University, Sokoto.
He began his career in civil service in 1998 with the Zamfara State Ministry for Local Government and Chieftaincy Affairs, eventually rising to the rank of Senior Higher Executive Officer Administration. His political career started in school, where he held various leadership positions. He joined partisan politics in 2013 with the All Nigeria Peoples Party (ANPP), which later merged into the All Progressives Congress (APC). He was elected to the 8th National Assembly in 2015 and re-elected for another term, although the elections were later nullified by the apex court.
Dr. Zubairu is known for his significant contributions to human and social development in Zamfara State and Northern Nigeria, including efforts against banditry, sponsoring medical treatments, supporting religious activities, and providing scholarships. He has received numerous awards and traditional titles for his service. He is also the chairman/CEO of ASAD Universal Concept Ltd and other businesses. Dr. Zubairu is happily married and blessed with children.

UMAR M. BAWA

STATE CHAIRMAN

Umar M. Bawa was born on January 1, 1998, in Tsafe Local Government Area of Zamfara State, Nigeria. He began his political journey in 2012 under the mentorship of the late Engr. Aliyu Idris Lalo (Durbin Gusau).
Before the death of his mentor, Umar continued the political movement and became the Chairman of the Lalo Social Media Organization (LASMO) in 2017.
His dedication and leadership later led him to play an active role in the political movement of Hon. Kabiru Amadu (Mai Palace), known for its slogan “Allah Shi Zai Bamu.” During this campaign, he served as the State Vice Chairman.
In 2019, Umar was appointed as the Secretary of the Mai Palace Public Awareness Committee (MPAC), where he contributed to grassroots mobilization and political awareness.
By 2022, he joined the political campaign of Governor Bello Mohammed Matawalle, serving as the Secretary of Matawalle New Media Promoters, a group focused on digital political engagement and media strategy.
That same year, he also became the Secretary of the 7-7 For Matawalle 2 Times Organization, demonstrating his commitment to strengthening the governor’s re-election efforts.
In 2022, Umar took another leadership step by leading a new initiative under the Hon. Yazeed Shehu Danfulani (Commissioner of Commerce), where he served as the Director General of the Yazeed Enlightenment Forum.
From 2023 to the present, he has been at the forefront of establishing and leading the Yazeed Trustee Organization, a political and social group focused on community development, youth empowerment, and public enlightenment.
Umar M. Bawa is a young, dynamic, and visionary political leader from Zamfara State with extensive experience in media coordination, political mobilization, and youth engagement. Through his various leadership roles, he continues to inspire and empower young people to participate actively in politics and governance.
